Case Summary
Overview
On the night of 14th March, Rohan Verma hosted a private celebration at an off-campus villa. The gathering comprised close friends, academic collaborators, and selected invitees from his immediate social circle. What began as a routine end-of-semester celebration gradually deteriorated into a night defined by rising tension, public confrontation, and concealed hostility between several attendees.
Incident Reconstruction
Events on the night unfolded in a sequence that, in retrospect, points toward a premeditated act rather than an impulsive one. The reconstruction below is based on witness statements, photographic evidence, and digital data extracted from recovered devices.
11:45 PM — A public argument erupted between Rohan and Megha. Multiple attendees later described the atmosphere as hostile. Several guests deliberately distanced themselves from the victim in the aftermath.
12:00 AM — The group assembled for a cake cutting. Photographic evidence independently confirms the presence of all five primary suspects at this precise moment.
12:05 AM — A threatening message was sent from the victim's device to a suspect. This communication was later deleted — and subsequently recovered via forensic extraction.
12:20 AM — A sudden, unexplained power outage occurred. Duration: approximately five minutes. All indoor surveillance and ambient lighting was lost. The exact movements of all individuals during this window remain unverified.
12:30 AM — Drinks were distributed among guests. Forensic analysis would later confirm that a toxic substance had been introduced into a single glass — the victim's. This moment is now considered the primary point of lethal intervention.
1:15 AM — Rohan Verma was last seen alive, observed alone. No further confirmed interaction with any individual has been established.
2:10 AM — Rohan Verma was discovered unconscious in his bedroom. No external injuries. No signs of forced entry. No evidence of a physical altercation.
Original Classification
Filed as: Accidental / Undetermined Cause of Death
Grounds for Reopening
Conflicting and internally inconsistent witness statements across the 12:30–2:10 AM window
Deleted digital communications recovered between the victim and a named suspect
Toxicological confirmation of deliberate poison ingestion — entry via liquid
Physical evidence — fingerprint match — directly linking a suspect to the victim's glass
The cumulative weight of this evidence points toward a planned act of poisoning, executed under the social cover of a private gathering.
